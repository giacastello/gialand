ABOUT

Gia is an Ecuadorian Media and Entertainment manager based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Trained in both artistic and business skills, her work covers a vast array of fields and interests. She is the Founder of Book Surfing Argentina, ex Editor in Chief for TKM US Mag, Community Leader for Startup Weekend Buenos Aires, and invited mentor for the Naves program in IAE Business School. In addition, she is Co Producer for the international film festival FICIC, and most recently her love for stories and her passion for trendspotting has led her into the transmedia scene, where she joined Periodismo Modelado, ÑoñoVR and SeirenFilms. Today, is also a front end developer in training at ADA ITW.
